# Sales FAQ

## Is this a full SaaS product?
No. This bundle is a complete local reference product plus research and sales collateral.

## What is the strongest claim?
Deterministic, replayable memory behavior validated on a frozen benchmark suite.

## What is not yet claimed?
Multi-tenant hosting, audited security, or customer-scale uptime.

## How should it be sold now?
As an evaluation kit, design-partner package, or research-license artifact.

## Why is provenance important?
It lets the buyer inspect where a durable fact came from and what superseded it.

## Why is this better than retrieval-only memory?
Retrieval-only stores text. Core LM stores current truth state plus lineage.
